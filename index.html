About Me

I've always enjoyed creation. Everything from woodworking, to making videos, coding websites, and even making up stories about playing tamborine for a band. In college I studied industrial design and much of my extracuricular time was spent doing improv and theater.

After I graduated from Walla Walla University in 2012, I got a job as a woodworker and eventually a position as an industrial designer. For the past few years I've been thinking more and more about designing for the digital space. So in early 2018 I left my job and enrolled in Alchemy Code Lab to learn coding. I made this website in the first week of class.

Explore the site to learn a little more about me.
